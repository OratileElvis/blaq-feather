About Us

Our Journey

BLAQfeather Tattoos was established in 2017 by the tatoo artist, Payne. Payne having always had a deep appreciation of art, developed a fascination for art on skin, and this grew into a love of tattoos. That love is what drives BLAQfeather to provide high-quality services to all its clients.

Our Style

At Blaq Feather Tattoos, we bring ink to life with a unique blend of bold creativity and fine-line precision. Our artistry ranges from delicate floral compositions and geometric designs to striking black-and-gray realism. Whether you're looking for elegant ornamental patterns, lifelike nature-inspired tattoos, or bold statement pieces, we craft every design with passion and detail. We specialize in black-and-gray work, offering both intricate linework and soft shading to create timeless pieces. From symbolic tattoos that tell a story to bold, modern designs that stand out, our style is as unique as the people who wear it.
